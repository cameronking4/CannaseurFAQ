  • Basics

  • Why sign up with email?

    Create an account to store your journals for safe keeping and access on any device. Notebooks, Journal entries and content will be saved to your account.

  • How do I sign up?

    Download & Register via the iOS app :)

  • What are notebooks?

    Cannaseur allows you to have multiple notebooks to organize your strains however you see fit. For example, if you want to keep track of your strains year by year you can have a notebook for "2020", or a notebook for each strain type Hybrid, Sativa or Indica, or by brand, location, etc. There's unlimited notebook options.

  • How do tags work?

    When creating a journal entry, you can tag suggested items to sort and filter your entries within all notebooks.

  • Jounral Entries

  • What can I enter?

    Cannaseur autocompletes with every strain name documented by Leafly.com. Enter a title or select from the list, enter notes or details and tag labels for future reference.

  • How do I search?

    You can filter by label using the top left icon on the Entries screen or search entry titles, typically the strain name.

  • Can I share my journals?

    Yes! You can share your jounrals with friends using their username. It will share it automatically in their notebooks.m
